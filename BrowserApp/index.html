What this app does

This is the league entry point for casual football leagues with invite-only joining.

Proper invitation links Owners and admins share a direct invite URL. The player lands on the invitation page immediately.
Multi-league accounts One account can belong to any number of leagues, and the lobby keeps them all together.
League admin controls League owners can promote members to admin or return admins back to members.
Admin-managed backups Full backup and restore is restricted to global admins for safety.
